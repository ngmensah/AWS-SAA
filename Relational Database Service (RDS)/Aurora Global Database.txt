Aurora Global Database

    - Good for Cross-Region Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity
    - Good for Global read scaling - offers low latency performance improvements
    - about 1 second or less replication between regions
    - Replication is only 1 way - from primary to secondary region
    - No impact on DB performance
    - secondary regions can have 16 replicas
    - can be promoted to Read/write
    - Currently MAX 5 secondary regions
